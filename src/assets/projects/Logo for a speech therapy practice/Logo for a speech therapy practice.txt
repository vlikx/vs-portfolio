Project: MUNDBILD – Visual Identity

Brand Strategy & Design
- The Brief: Holistic branding for a newly founded logopedic practice, requiring a versatile logo that works across digital and physical touchpoints.
- The Concept: Developed a sophisticated combination mark (icon + typography) that flows seamlessly—symbolizing communication and professional care.
- Aesthetic: A minimalist, high-contrast blue and black palette designed to convey both medical trust and modern clarity.

Technical Execution
- Versatility: Engineered the visual assets in Adobe Illustrator to ensure infinite scalability for diverse applications—from large-scale glass frosting and signage to social media icons and business cards.
- Client Synergy: Led the creative process in close collaboration with the founder, ensuring the final visual language aligns perfectly with the practice’s identity and professional goals.
- Impact: Delivered a cohesive "Vision + Value" branding package that establishes a strong, professional market presence for the new practice.

Project: INTERACTIVE WEDDING INVITATION

Concept & Innovation
- The Brief: Create a custom, high-end wedding invitation for a friend featuring a unique "Sliding Reveal" mechanism.
- The Idea: Designed an interactive physical experience where sliding the card open physically moves the bride illustration toward the groom, symbolizing their union.
-Aesthetic: Elegant floral themes combined with custom character illustrations, blending traditional warmth with modern interactive design.

Technical Execution
- Full-Cycle Design: Handled everything from initial character assets and layout to final print-ready production files.
- Production Management: Managed the end-to-end process, including rigorous color proofing and direct coordination with professional print services for final delivery.
- Vision + Value: Balanced intricate manual craftsmanship with professional print data standards to deliver a high-quality, personalized product that exceeded client expectations.
